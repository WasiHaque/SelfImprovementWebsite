Welcome to our Mental Health section!

Mental health is a vital component of overall well-being, influencing how we think, feel, and act. Mental health affects our ability to handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions. Just like physical health, maintaining good mental health requires effort and attention. Our mental health section provides valuable resources and strategies to help you nurture your mind and achieve a balanced, fulfilling life.
The first step is recognising you need help, which is why we have made a little quiz for you to assess yourselves, found under the **Emotional Well-being** module.

Stress Management

Ways to manage your stress

1. Mediation

Spend a few minutes each day practicing meditation. Focus on your breath and let go of any distracting thoughts.

2. Deep Breathing:

Practice deep breathing exercises to calm your mind and reduce stress. Inhale deeply through your nose, hold for a few seconds, and exhale slowly through your mouth.

3. Mindful Walking

Take a walk and focus on the sensations of your steps, the sounds around you, and the feeling of the air on your skin.
